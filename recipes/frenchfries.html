French Fries

Description

My family loves seasoned fries and with 4 children the bag of fries from the store became too expensive. So I tried batter â€” after all, it makes chicken, shrimp, and onion rings crispy so why not potatoes! Now even my husband can't get enough of these French fries. They even stay crispy when you reheat them in the microwave.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Paprika
  • Water

Steps

  1. Slice the potatoes and place into a bowl of water
  2. Heat oil in pan
  3. While oil is heating, sift flour and salt into a bowl. Gradually add in water to make batter
  4. Drain fries and pat try, dip into batter
  5. Lower fries into oil, fry until golden and crispy brown
  6. Drain fries on a paper towel plate
  7. Serve and enjoy
